Judi Azmand


--------- About

Hello there! I'm Judi.

Originally from Vancouver, Canada, I made my way to Tokyo, Japan in 2018 straight out of university. Before getting my feet on the ground in Japan, I had a unique journey through high school and university filled with an abundance of discovery.


I've ventured into a multitude of fields including video game journalism, psychology/cognitive science research, and programming/web development. At the moment, I'm an assistant language teacher teaching English to highschool students in Tokyo while studying Japanese, as well as building various coding projects.


I'm very rarily uninterested in something, but the 4 pillars that peak my interest the most are video games, research, technology, and languages. I consider the combination of these pillars to be somewhere/something where human-computer interaction is of high importance.
