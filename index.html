Judi Azmand


--------- About

Hello there! I'm Judi.

Originally from Vancouver, Canada, I made my way to Tokyo, Japan in 2018 straight out of university.


I've ventured into a multitude of fields including video game journalism, psychology/cognitive science research, and programming/web development. At the moment, i’m a software engineer at a Yokohama-based company, and working on side coding projects during my free time.


I'm very rarely uninterested in something, but the four pillars that uphold my interest the most are video games, research, technology, and languages. I consider the combination of these pillars to be somewhere/something where human-computer interaction is of high importance.
